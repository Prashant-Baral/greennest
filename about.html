Our Story

Born from a belief that living sustainably shouldn't mean sacrificing beauty or comfort.

Why GreenNest Exists

In 2020, our founders Priya and Arun were frustrated. They wanted to make sustainable choices for their home — but the options were either ugly, overpriced, or "greenwashed." They decided to fix that.

GreenNest was built on a simple charter: every product must be genuinely sustainable, honestly priced, and beautiful enough to live with forever. No compromises.

Today we serve 45,000+ homes across India, with a team of 28 passionate people working from our Bangalore office.
